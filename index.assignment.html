Get ready, Stockholm!

HYROX returns to Sweden’s capital for four epic days of fitness racing at HYROX Stockholm 25/26 from December 18th to 21st!

Whether you’re an experienced competitor or stepping into the arena for the first time, HYROX Stockholm is your chance to push your limits, break boundaries, and discover what you’re truly capable of.

The choice is yours: go solo in the HYROX Open or Pro division, team up with a friend in HYROX Doubles or Doubles Pro, or gather a squad and take on the challenge together in the HYROX Relay division!
